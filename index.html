About

I hold a B.S. in Environmental Science and Policy from University of Maryland College Park, where I also minored in Atmospheric Science. Following a year of service, I am excited to pursue a PhD and return to conducting research analyzing geospatial climate data for applications related to climate change adaptation, severe weather, agricultural conditions, and/or institutional and community disaster response and recovery.
